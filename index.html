                                PythonPunk

    is a text-based RP pick your story game currently being developed by Nathan Stevens(A.K.A TheTechPunk) and Matthew Royster(A.K.A MattRoyster) For more info on development follow @The_TechPunk and check the GitHub Repository

                                   INFO

    In a world of War there is a need, a need for a Hero. Be that hero in PythonPunk be the Captain and save the worlds of the United Planets! Several different paths for you to take resulting in a different Story every time!

    Intro in C0DE:
