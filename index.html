PythonPunk is a text-based RP pick your story game currently being developed by Nathan Stevens(A.K.A TheTechPunk) and Matthew Royster(A.K.A MattRoyster) For more info on development follow @The_TechPunk and check the GitHub Repository

Goal

To make a game that is free to all to enjoy

Lore

In a world of War there is a need, a need for a Hero. Be that hero in PythonPunk be the Captain and save the worlds of the United Planets!. Several different paths for you to take resulting in a different Story every time!

Intro: print("In a world before our own") time.sleep(3) print("There was a devastating war") time.sleep(3) print("A rebellion continued to fight for the fallen army") time.sleep(3) print("But in order to prevent the army from falling") time.sleep(3) print("They needed him...a hero...the leader of the rebels") In code!
